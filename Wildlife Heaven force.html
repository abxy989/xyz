Welcome to Wildlife Heaven Force

The Social Purpose IT Company: With our Direct Actions,we fight problems where authorities fail. Wildlife Heaven Force is an anti-poaching unit that saves Wildlife and Nature in Southern Africa from extinction.. Our Mission "To inspire communities to protect nature and wildlife through education, advocacy, and conservation efforts, fostering biodiversity and a sustainable future."



OUR THREE STEPS APPROACH TO PROTECTING WILDLIFE AND NATURE

Education and Awareness Campaigns

By increasing awareness, people become more conscious of their impact on the environment and wildlife, fostering a culture of conservation and responsible behavior and implement educational programs aimed at raising awareness about the importance of biodiversity, conservation efforts, and sustainable practices.

Conservation and Habitat Restoration Projects:

Directly contribute to the preservation of biodiversity, ensuring that ecosystems can thrive and support
wildlife populations over the long term.Allocate resources towards conservation projects that protect
endangered species, preserve habitats, and restore ecosystems.

Advocacy and Policy Influence:

By shaping policy, the charity can create systemic change that supports conservation efforts and ensures
legal protections for vulnerable species and habitats.Advocate for stronger environmental policies and
regulations at local, national, and international levels.
